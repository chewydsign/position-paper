Seeds of My Design Journey

Over the past two years, my design journey has pivoted toward sustainable design, by joining the iHOG research team led by Jack Brandsma and acquiring an internship position at Studio Tjeerd Veenhoven. Not only do I think it is crucial for designers of our generation to be conscious about the things we create, but I also find material research to be extremely interesting.
